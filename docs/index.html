About Me

I am pursuing PhD under the guidance of Prof B Sundar Rajan in the Department of Electrical Communication Engineering at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru.

I received the B.Tech degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Jawaharlal Nehru Government Engineering College, Sundernagar, HP.

After securing AIR 10 in the GATE (EC) 2018, I joined ECE department of IISc Bangalore, for M.Tech in Signal Processing. In August 2019, I upgraded to PhD which I am currently pursuing.

Broad Goal of Research

I am working on reducing the cost of privacy in distributed communication systems, e.g. cache-aided content delivery networks, distributed computations and information delivery networks.

Research Topics

  • Coded Caching
  • Private Information Retrieval
  • Private Information Delivery
  • Distributed Computing
